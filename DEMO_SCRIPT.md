# 3-Minute Live Demo Script


## Demo Flow (Exactly 3 minutes)

### [0:00–0:15] Introduction

"Meet Amara. She's a 28-year-old accountant in Lagos earning $8K/year. 
She wants to upskill into tech to increase her income. But she's paralyzed 
by choice: What should she learn? Will there be jobs? Is it worth 6 months 
of study?

That's where Learning Path Validator comes in."

### [0:15–0:45] Home Page
**Action:**
- Point at home page
- Say: "She chooses her desired path..."
- Click dropdown → Select "Product Management"
- Click second dropdown → Select "Nigeria"


"She wants to learn Product Management in Nigeria where she lives. 
Now let's validate if this path actually leads to jobs in her region."

### [0:45–2:00]  Results & Explainability
**Action:**
- Click "Validate Path"
- Wait for results to load
- Scroll slowly through each section

**At validation badge (0:50–1:00):**
"STRONG validation. 73% confidence. This means the data shows it's a solid path."

**At job demand section (1:00–1:20):**
"Here's why: There are 340 open Product Manager roles in Nigeria. 
That's up 22% from last year. Strong demand."

**P salary section (1:20–1:35):**
"Entry-level salaries range from $18K to $35K per year. 
That's more than double what Amara earns now."

**Psources (1:35–1:50):**
"Notice: we show our sources. LinkedIn, Glassdoor, World Bank data. 
Every number is traceable. That's transparency."

**P alternatives (1:50–2:00):**
"If she wanted higher salary, she could consider Data Analytics or Web Dev. 
But Product Management is strong for her region right now."

### [2:00–2:45] UI & Safety
**Action:**
- Scroll back to top
- Show disclaimer


"We're transparent about limitations. This is AI-assisted, not a guarantee. 
But it gives Amara real data to make a confident decision."

**Go back to home, select different path (2:20–2:45):**
"Let's try another path. Data Analytics in Kenya."
- Select "Data Analytics"
- Select "Kenya"
- Click validate

### [2:45–3:00] Close

"Amara now has the data she needs. She knows Product Management has jobs 
in Nigeria, realistic salary expectations, and exactly what skills to learn. 
She completes the course with confidence.

3 months later: She lands her first PM role at a fintech startup. 
$20K/year. 150% raise. And she tells her network. That's the impact."
